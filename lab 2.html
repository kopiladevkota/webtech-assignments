Welcome to the BSc.CSIT Department

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc.CSIT) program, affiliated with Tribhuvan University, is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and information technology. The program emphasizes both theoretical and practical aspects of computing, preparing students to meet the challenges of the rapidly evolving tech industry.

5th Semester Subjects

  • System Analysis & Design
  • Web Technology
  • Simulation & Modeling
  • Design and Analysis of Algorithms
  • Cryptography
  • Multimedia
